About Phi Mu

Since 1852, Phi Mu Fraternity has challenged women to become their personal best. We are proud of our rich history as the second-oldest secret society for women in the U.S. and we continually strive to provide our members with the many benefits of a steadfast sisterhood, embodying our open motto, “Les Soeurs Fideles,” the Faithful Sisters.

Our Philanthropy

Since 1986, Phi Mu has raised over $20 million for Children’s Miracle Network Hospitals and in 2019, we were recognized as a CMN Hospitals Miracle Million Club member, an honor for corporate partners that raise more than $1 million each year. In 2018, Phi Mu announced our commitment to raise $10 million for CMN Hospitals in five years which was completed 6 months early!

Our Sisterhood

Phi Mu is a family where everyone is welcomed with open arms. Our goal is to provide young women a sense of home away from home. We have the strongest and most fun sisterhood out there!
